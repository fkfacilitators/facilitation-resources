# 사단법인 한국퍼실리테이터연합회 (Korea Facilitators Association · fKF)

Open facilitation guides and resources maintained by the Korea Facilitators Association (fKF), a registered non-profit incorporated association in South Korea, established in 2015.

## About fKF

fKF is a non-governmental, non-partisan, non-profit organization focused on education and capacity-building in facilitation. We train and certify public-interest facilitators, run educational programs and youth discussion classes, publish facilitation learning materials, and support a nationwide network of facilitators across eight regional chapters.

- Website: https://fkf.or.kr
- Education platform: https://eduspeaks.kr

## About this repository

This repository is the home for fKF's open, freely reusable facilitation resources, such as facilitation method guides, workshop templates and worksheets, and training materials for public-interest facilitators. Materials are added and updated on an ongoing basis.
